Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 6, 2025

1. General

Orbit Connect is a Telegram bot providing technical access to a secure node. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any technical data processed by the service.

2. Data Collected

  • Telegram ID and username (if available)
  • Device type
  • License validity period
  • Payment status via Telegram Stars (no financial data)
  • Support history (if applicable)

3. Purpose

All data is processed solely to ensure connection functionality, service stability, and support operations.

4. Retention

  • Licenses: up to 24 months after expiration
  • Support tickets: up to 12 months
  • Technical logs: up to 90 days

5. Third Parties

Data is not shared with third parties except for Telegram (platform) and hosting providers (infrastructure).

6. Deletion

You may request data removal via the /delete_me command in the bot or by emailing orbitconnect@proton.me.

7. Contact

Email: orbitconnect@proton.me
